I'm a demographer and quantitative social scientist.
My research interests lie at the intersection of networks, demography, and quantitative methodology.

In the summer of 2015, I finished my Ph.D. in Demography at Princeton's Office of Population Research. I'm spending the fall of 2015, as a Research Scientist at Facebook, and in January 2016 I will start as an Assistant Professor of Demography at the University of California, Berkeley.
